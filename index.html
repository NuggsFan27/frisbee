About The Band

The Band CAMINO is an American pop-rock band from Memphis, Tennessee, known for their emotionally charged lyrics and catchy, guitar-driven sound. Blending elements of indie rock, pop, and alternative, they’ve gained a dedicated fanbase with hits like “Daphne Blue” and “See Through,” earning a reputation for high-energy performances and relatable songwriting.
